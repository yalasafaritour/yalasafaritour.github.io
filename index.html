Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Yala safaris

What is the best time to visit Yala National Park?

The best time for wildlife viewing in Yala is from February to June when water levels are low and animals congregate around water sources. However, Yala offers excellent wildlife sightings throughout the year.

What should I bring on a safari?

We recommend bringing: comfortable clothing in neutral colors, a hat, sunglasses, sunscreen, insect repellent, binoculars, camera with zoom lens, and any personal medications. For morning safaris, a light jacket is advisable as it can be cool at dawn.

Are children allowed on safaris?

Yes, children of all ages are welcome on our safaris. Children under 5 can join free of charge. Please note that all children must be accompanied by an adult and should remain seated during the safari for safety.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer free cancellation up to 48 hours before your scheduled safari for a full refund. Cancellations between 24-48 hours receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 24 hours are non-refundable. In case of park closure due to weather or other unforeseen circumstances, we offer alternative dates or full refunds.
